Towsnville was established in 1954 by Abe Froman. Abe Froman grew up in the suburbs of Chicago. He was the son of a butcher who specialized in sausage links. He grew up in an unfair and unjust world. His dream was to have a town that was self sufficient, had no poverty, and was well organized in resources.

Mayor Jared Fogle

Located in the heart of Someder County, Transylvania. Townsville offers its citizens and guests a balanced suburban community. From lake communities loaded with recreational facilities and museums, an excellent modern free public library, exceptional public school system, to shopping and business centers, Towsnville has something for everyone. Our site provides informational services about township departments and divisions, history, recreation and more!
